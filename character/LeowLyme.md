<h2>
👀 appearance
</h2>
<table><tr><td>

* he has a head which is attached on a motorized shaft.
* he has 2 parallel tracks on each side, each track has 4 wheels.
* he has a cannon in front of his face. 
* he has 2 square eyes on the sides of his head.
* he has 2 triangle ears on top of his head, each one face opposite to each other.
* he has a bracket from back of his head.
* he has a tail with 4 bones, each bones are attached with universal joints. the tail-tip is cropped-shape.

<h2>
<img src="https://github.com/dujanhung/sicalyme-svg/blob/icon/setting.svg" width="64" height="64">
functionality
</h2>
<table><tr><td>

* he don't need a driver, because he is controlled by an offline AI system.
* he can communicate to human. although he can't speak, he can use other ways to communicate.
* his neck can twist around freely, with motor for source force, and absorber for stability.
* his cannon can bend up and down, to be able to arm at airborne targets.
* he can jump, using pistons inside the tank tracks as source force, and absorber for movement stability.
* his tank tracks can move forward and backward as both tracks go to the same direction; it can turn left and right as each tracks go opposite to each other; however, it can't strafe left and right, unless it's replaced with external steering components.
* his cannon can shoot bullets. it's not only a long-range weapon, but also a combat weapon when used in a short distance.
* his eye can see colors as well as human's eye. however, he cannot see a same object with both eyes.
* his hearing ability can detect the low and high frequency sounds.
* his bracket is mainly used as a passenger seat for a human. also, it can store other items, and liquid. however, long-term liquid contact is harmful, as it eat though the bracket over time.
* his tail is mainly used for balance. also, it can grasp items.

</td></tr></table>
</td></tr></table>
